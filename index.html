The Types of People We Find

From rising stars to practice leaders, we know where to find the right coastal engineering talent.

Senior Technical Leaders

Practice leads, technical directors, and senior engineers who can mentor your team and win work. Usually PE licensed with 10+ years of coastal experience.

Project Managers

PMs who understand coastal work isn't just civil engineering. They know about tide windows, environmental windows, and why marine contractors are different.

Modeling Specialists

Engineers who can set up and run complex numerical models. Whether it's MIKE 21, SWAN, or ADCIRC, they know how to make the software sing.

Design Engineers

The people who design the structures. Marine structures, coastal protection, ports and terminals. They understand wave forces, soil-structure interaction, and corrosion.

Rising Stars

EITs and junior engineers on the PE track. Smart people who chose coastal engineering and want to build their careers in the field.

Specialty Roles

Permitting specialists, dredging engineers, coastal resilience planners. The niche experts who make complex projects possible.
